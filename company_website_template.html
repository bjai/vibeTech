About vibeTech

vibeTech is a leading financial infrastructure platform connecting institutions to banks & leading financial rails worldwide. Our clients are banks, asset managers, investment funds, fintechs and corporates in more than 60 countries. Our platform acts as a single gateway connecting our clients to their banks, their correspondents and global financial networks such as Swift, enabling reliable international payments, FX deals, trade finance, and securities settlement. With a strong reputation in the industry, vibeTech established itself as the UNIQUE player bringing the most innovative services in financial messaging to its customers worldwide.
